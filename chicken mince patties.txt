Chicken mince patties. 

500g chicken mince. 
1 egg. 
Spring onions. 
De-seeded chilli or capsicum. 
Coriander and/or Basil. 
Half cup of breadcrumbs. 
Beetroot to taste (adds tang).
Garlic. 

Blend or chop all large ingredients. 
Combine all in bowl. 
Heat oil (1/2 cup or so all up) in pan. 
Cook teaspoon sized amounts of mixture, 2 minute per side. 
Let cool on absorbent paper.

Serve with salad or potato salad.
